Stephen Curry: The Greatest Shooter Ever

Early Years & Rise (2009–2014)

  • Drafted 7th overall in 2009 by the Golden State Warriors
  • Set the rookie record for 3-pointers made
  • Known for ball handling and long-range accuracy
  • Overcame early ankle injuries

MVP Seasons & Championship Runs (2014–2019)

  1. Back-to-back MVP Awards
    • 2015 MVP
    • 2016 Unanimous MVP
  2. Championship Wins
    • 2015 – vs Cavaliers
    • 2017 – vs Cavaliers
    • 2018 – vs Cavaliers
  3. Impact on the Game
    • Revolutionized deep 3-point shooting
    • Spaced the floor like never before
    • Changed how teams defend

Later Career & Legacy (2020–Present)

  • 2022 Finals MVP after defeating Celtics
  • NBA's all-time leader in 3-pointers made
  • Continues to play at elite level into mid-30s
  • Global icon and inspiration to next generation
